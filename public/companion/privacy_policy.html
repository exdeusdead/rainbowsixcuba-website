Extension Privacy Policy

Companion Privacy

This policy applies specifically to the Rainbow Six CUBA Companion Chrome Extension and related companion tools.

1. Purpose

The Rainbow Six CUBA Companion Extension is designed for community statistics, profile assistance, and future integration with Rainbow Six CUBA services. It is intended to support community tools, not unrelated browsing activity.

2. Data We May Process

Depending on available features, the extension may process limited community-related information such as public usernames, public profile references, community identifiers, extension settings, and data needed to communicate with Rainbow Six CUBA services.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

The extension is not designed to collect passwords, payment information, private messages, unrelated browsing history, personal financial data, or sensitive personal information unrelated to the community purpose.

4. Use of Data

Data is used to provide companion functionality, improve reliability, support community statistics, connect with Rainbow Six CUBA services, and prepare future public profile or ranking features when available.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be transmitted only to Rainbow Six CUBA controlled services, such as api.rainbowsixcuba.com, when required for companion functionality.

6. Transparency

Any future feature that changes the type of data processed by the extension should be documented transparently before or when released.

7. Contact

For privacy or support requests, contact Rainbow Six CUBA staff through the official Discord or future official support email listed on the website.

Important notice

Rainbow Six CUBA is an independent fan community and is not affiliated, associated, authorized or endorsed by Ubisoft or Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six Siege. This extension and related tools are community projects. Community 100% apolitical.
