Privacy Policy

Rainbow Six CUBA

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Rainbow Six CUBA is an independent fan community and is not affiliated, associated, authorized, sponsored, or endorsed by Ubisoft or Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six Siege. This Privacy Policy explains how our community website, Discord systems, and companion tools may collect, use, and protect information.

Community position: Rainbow Six CUBA is a 100% apolitical gaming community. Any visual content, fictional references, tactical scenes, or country-inspired themes are used only for community entertainment and gaming atmosphere.

1. Information We May Collect

Depending on the features used, Rainbow Six CUBA may collect limited information such as Discord ID, Ubisoft username or account identifier, FACEIT information, public game statistics, region or country, preferred language, verification status, team or coach role, and information voluntarily submitted through forms or tickets.

2. How We Use Information

Information may be used to verify community members, organize teams and events, display public statistics or leaderboards, improve community tools, provide support, prevent abuse, and maintain server integrity.

3. Public and Private Data

Some information, such as usernames, public game statistics, rankings, team affiliation, coach status, or verified partner status, may be visible to the community. Sensitive verification details are handled with restricted access when possible.

4. Chrome Extension and Companion Tools

The Rainbow Six CUBA Companion extension and related tools are designed for community and statistics purposes. They do not intentionally collect passwords, payment information, private messages, or unrelated browsing data. Any future extension features will be documented transparently.

5. Cookies and Analytics

The website may use basic analytics, hosting logs, or performance tools to understand usage, improve reliability, and protect the service. We do not sell personal information.

6. Third-Party Services

The community may rely on third-party platforms such as Discord, GitHub, Cloudflare, FACEIT, Ubisoft-related public services, or other gaming platforms. Their own privacy policies apply when you use their services.

7. Data Retention

Community data may be retained while a member remains active or while needed for moderation, verification, support, statistics, or safety. Users may request correction or removal when applicable.

8. User Rights and Requests

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their community data by contacting Rainbow Six CUBA support. Some records may be retained when required for moderation, security, or abuse prevention.

9. Children and Minors

Rainbow Six CUBA is intended for users who meet the minimum age requirements of Discord, Ubisoft, FACEIT, and relevant community rules. Additional protections may apply for minors.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, support, or data requests, contact us through the official Rainbow Six CUBA Discord or future official support email listed on the website.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the website, Discord systems, API, statistics bot, or companion tools evolve. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
